About The Studio

The acclaimed Japanese animation film studio, Studio Ghibli by animators and directors Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata and producer Yasuyoshi Tokuma.
The studio is known for the high quality of its filmmaking and its artistry, amassing over 120 awards.

Studio Ghibli Inc. (Japanese: 株式会社スタジオジブリ) is a Japanese animation film studio headquartered in Koganei, Tokyo.It is best known for its animated feature films, and has also produced several short subjects, television commercials, and two television films. Its mascot and most recognizable symbol is a character named Totoro, a giant catlike spirit from the 1988 anime film My Neighbor Totoro. Among the studio's highest-grossing films are Spirited Away (2001), Howl's Moving Castle (2004) and Ponyo (2008). The studio was founded on June 15, 1985 by directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata and producer Toshio Suzuki, after the successful performance of Topcraft's Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (1984). It has also collaborated with video game studios on the visual development of several games.

Movies
Howl's Moving Castle

After the protagonist, young Sophie is cursed by a witch and turned into an old woman she sets out to seek her fortune, which takes her to Howl's strange moving castle.
This gets her caught up in Howl's resistance to fight another kingdom in the kings name.

Princess Mononoke

While protecting his village from a cursed boar-god, a confident young warrior, Ashitaka, is stricken by it's deadly curse. So he seeks a cure from the deer-like god Shishigami.
In his travels, he sees humans ravaging the earth, bringing down the wrath of wolf god Moro and his human companion Princess Mononoke.
Ashitaka attempts to broker peace between her and the humans but brings only conflict.

Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki's Delivery Service tells the story of Kiki, a little witch who has to leave home for a year when she turns 13 to begin her solo witch training.
Accompanied by Jiji, her talking cat, and her broomstick, she searches for a new town where she can settle and find out who she is.

The Secret World of Arrietty

Arrietty and the rest of her family live in peaceful anonymity like most of their kind.
As they make their own home from items that they borrow from the humans houses.
However, life changes for her family when a human boy discovers Arrietty.

Spirited Away

After Chihiro and her parents stumble upon a seemingly abandoned amusement park they undergo a mysterious transformation. Chihiro must fend for herself as she encounters strange spirits, assorted creatures and a grumpy sorceress who seeks to prevent her from returning to the human world.

My Neighbor Totoro

This animated tale follows schoolgirl Satsuke and her younger sister, Mei, as they settle into an old country house with their father and wait for their mother to recover from an illness in an hospital nearby.
As the sisters explore their new home, they encounter and befriend playful spirits in their house and the nearby forest, most notably the massive cuddly creature known as Totoro.
